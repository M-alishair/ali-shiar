From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia


The second season of the American television series Loki, based on Marvel Comics featuring the character of the same name, It is set in the Marvel Cinematic Universe (MCU), sharing continuity with the films of the franchise. The season is produced by Marvel Studios, with Eric Martin serving as head writer and Justin Benson and Aaron Moorhead leading the directing team.
